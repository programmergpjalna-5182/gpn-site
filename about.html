About the Institute

Government Polytechnic, Nagpur is one of the leading technical institutes in Maharashtra established with the goal of providing high-quality technical education and fostering innovation.

The institute offers diploma programs in various engineering disciplines and is affiliated with MSBTE. With a dedicated faculty and modern infrastructure, GPN aims to produce skilled technicians ready to serve industry and society.
